Overview

To develop company's vision into a functional website, using the most up to date technologies and deploy to your own servers. The goal of this project is to create a website that showcases dfh's values and services as well as provide a seamless experience for its users.

\
Deliverables

The deliverables are as follows;

1) Custom design of the following pages;

  a) Homepage/landing page

  b) About DFH

  c) Conversations

  d) Blog

  e) Advertise with Us

  f) Events

  g) Contact

2) Bespoke development of the individual pages stated above.

3) Create a mail notification system for filled out contact form and newsletter opt-ins.

4) Synchronize blogposts with mailing list for newsletter notifications.

5) Optimize pages for fast access and host website to client's server.
